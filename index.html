Portfolio

Performing The Voice

Poster Project

Initiated poster project to bring awareness and engagement to the Speech Communication Department on courses offered at the University of Waterloo. Spotlight poster was designed using Canva, Lunapic, and Gimp. Included a QR code for user accessibility, directing them to the course site and description. The audience of the poster would be post-secondary students.

Infographic

Personal Project

After brainstorming several concepts for inforgraphics, I landed on the idea of providing an overview of the business products available at ArtBaddie. From start to end, this infographic details the process of creating our product for our consumers. The infographic primarily focuses on soft colours to invoke a sense of warmth while capturing the attention of the audience.
